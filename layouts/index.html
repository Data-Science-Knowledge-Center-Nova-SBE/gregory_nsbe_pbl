How it works

Step 1 Search for Multiple Sclerosis

Step 2 Index and organise

We use text analysis and machine learning to identify relevant articles to list on the home page.

Step 3 Filter and distribute

Every 2 days the admin team tags which articles show promise for new treatment and better quality of life for people with Multiple Sclerosis.

Every week the articles tagged as relevant for patients are compiled and sent to a mailing list of doctors, researchers, and physical therapists.
